About Us

Video from our latest project: International Mindedness

Creative opportunities is a not-for-profit organisation that aims to encourage artistic practices in order to benefit the community.

We run workshops within the Greater London area for young people who are at an artistic disadvantage.

We provide platforms for developing and promoting artworks that the participants create.
